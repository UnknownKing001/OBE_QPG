Transforming Assessment Through Intelligent Design

Our Mission

To revolutionize educational assessment by providing a sophisticated, AI-powered tool that creates question papers aligned with Outcome-Based Education (OBE) principles. By leveraging AI, we enable educators to design precise, comprehensive assessments that go beyond surface-level knowledge to measure higher-order skills like analysis, application, and critical thinking. This tool ensures that every question directly reflects the specific learning outcomes of a course, promoting more accurate and meaningful evaluations of student learning. Ultimately, our goal is to empower educators to create assessments that truly reflect the competencies students should acquire, moving away from traditional methods to offer targeted, effective evaluations.

OBE Implementation

  • Align questions directly with specific Course Outcomes (COs)
  • Map questions to Bloom's Taxonomy levels
  • Ensure balanced assessment across cognitive domains
  • Provide data-driven insights into student learning
